## Future Logo Ideas (for graphic designer)

- Main element: Stylized galaxy/cosmic swirl in cyan
- Typography: Modern sans-serif, bold
- Accent: Purple/pink gradient for cosmic effect
- Shape: Circular or hexagonal badge

- Main element: DNA double helix with circuit patterns
- Typography: Clean, tech-forward

- Shape: Circular with helix inside

## Brand Mark Guidelines

### Usage Priority
1. **Full ASCII Logo**: Terminal, CLI, README files
2. **Compact Mark**: Navigation bars, footers, small spaces
3. **Icon Only**: Favicons, app icons, minimal UI

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space of 1/2 the logo height around all logos

### Minimum Sizes
- ASCII Logo: Minimum 60 characters wide
- Icon: Minimum 32x32 pixels
- Text Logo: Minimum 120px wide

### Do's and Don'ts
✅ Use official color schemes
✅ Maintain aspect ratios
✅ Use on contrasting backgrounds
✅ Keep logos legible

❌ Don't distort or stretch
❌ Don't change colors arbitrarily
❌ Don't add effects or shadows (unless specified)
❌ Don't recreate logos
